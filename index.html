About the Festival

AnyFest is a two-day music festival bringing together fans of indie, rock, and electronic music. Held in the heart of the city, the festival celebrates emerging talent and well-known artists, offering a unique experience under the open sky. Join us for unforgettable performances, delicious food trucks, interactive art installations, and an atmosphere full of positive vibes. It's the perfect summer event for music lovers looking to discover new artists and enjoy the energy of a live crowd.

Location

AnyFest will take place at AnyLocation, a venue known for its spacious and vibrant atmosphere, surrounded by nature and close to the city’s best landmarks. The location is easily accessible by public transport, with plenty of parking areas nearby for those driving in. Whether you’re camping on-site or visiting for a single day, AnyLocation offers the ideal setting for a festival adventure. Explore the venue map to find different stages, food courts, chill-out zones, and merchandise stalls to enhance your festival experience.
